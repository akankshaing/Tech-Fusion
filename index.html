EVENTS

Diverse range of events,
Dynamic development challenges, High-stakes hacking competitions, Exhilarating gaming battles, and Innovative design contests,
Offering participants a platform to showcase their skills, creativity, and passion

HackXtreme

An adrenaline-filled hacking competition where ethical hackers race against time to solve complex cybersecurity problems, including penetration testing, exploit development, and vulnerability analysis, all while overcoming unexpected challenges.


Gaming Nexus

A thrilling gaming event featuring multiplayer team games and solo challenges across a variety of popular titles. It’s a space for both casual players and eSports competitors to showcase their gaming skills and experience intense competition.

InnovateX

A tech-focused event where developers bring their ideas to life through coding, app development, and creating prototypes. InnovateX encourages participants to push technological boundaries and collaborate on innovative solutions.

DesignVerse

A creative design event where participants explore graphic design, UX/UI, and digital art. It’s an opportunity for designers to tackle real-world challenges, create user-centric designs, and showcase their artistic talent.

PixelPerfect

A photography competition that celebrates the art of capturing powerful moments through the lens. Participants focus on creativity, composition, and storytelling, with categories ranging from landscape to portrait to abstract photography.
